Zachariae Group

Zachariae Group’s name is derived from the concept of a collaboration ,teamwork ,trust &reliability. These values form the core of our corporate personality.

Since its establishment, Zachariae Group has proven to be a dynamic and powerful contributor towards our invertors vision , Team overall development , and customer service through our sincere efforts.

We pursue our developmental path in order to achieve vision and mission in light of the path shown by our investors & veterans of the company through our Business plan for coming years . The framework of Zachariae new Business plan,focuses on the following objectives:

  • Creating value and achieving sustainable growth
  • Increasing operating incomes for all business segments
  • Building a solid capital structure for every segment
  • Building a strong financial position to ensure stable operational and developmental processes.
Every business segment is based on key goals, including focusing on the local market, collaborating with the channel partners , making the most outcome of assets, working on improving current operational efficiency and increasing revenues for every segment & subsidiaries.
