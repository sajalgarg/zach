Export Import

ZACHARIAE GROUP through its investment vehicle invests in various business domains as well as working in ecommerce segment by developing brands in various category segments and also works for the Import-Export of processed foods in domestic & international market.

Currently we are Exporting goods to destination JEBEL ALI-U.A.E & expanding our services in Australia and Russia through our various products categories .

Our IMPORT includes dry fruits ,food supplements,furniture products , capitals goods involving electronics , IT , computer & mobile accessories .

Our EXPORT includes fresh Fruits & Vegetables such as onion, pomegranate, Apple & other seasonal products, mango pulp, peanut butter, tomato ketchup ,natural honey,mineral water,grains & other processed food products.

All of our Fresh Vegetables and Fresh Fruits are grown by farmers in natural conditions and are handpicked, cultivated without using any harmful substances i.e. pesticides and chemicals.

Fresh Fresh Fruits & Vegetables are packed in a quality packaging material for elongated freshness.
